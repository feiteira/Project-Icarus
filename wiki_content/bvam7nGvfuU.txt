If Israeli leaders are sitting in rooms saying Iran is months away from a nuclear  weapon, then why hasn't Israel just ended it? Why no invasion? Why no ground troops? Why, with  all of that military capability, has everything stayed in the air and at a distance? And look,  I get why people ask that. On the surface, it seems almost obvious. If you have a mortal  enemy and you have one of the most capable militaries in the world and you believe the clock  is ticking, why not finish it? But the moment you actually start pulling on that thread, you realize  the answer is not what most people expect. It's not about hesitation.
