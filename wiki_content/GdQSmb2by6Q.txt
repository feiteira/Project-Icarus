== Overview ==

Why is a soft-spoken educator from Beijing suddenly being hailed by some as a modern-day Nostradamus? In early 20200, a single dense academic lecture by Professor Jang Shu Chin ignited the internet, cutting through the noise of traditional political commentary. While most analysts were caught off guard by shifting global alliances and sudden conflicts, Jen seemed to have expected them. His fame isn't built on mysticism or crystal balls, but on a methodology so cold and calculated that it borders on the clinical. In this exploration, we dive into the mind of the architect behind predictive history to understand how he decodes the chaotic atmosphere of our modern world. Jen's framework, which he calls predictive history, is far removed from the emotional rhetoric of cable news. It is an intellectual structure built on three unbreakable pillars.
