## Project Icarus — Research Day (Apr 24, continued)

### Key Finding: 10× Thrust Target — DIY Ceiling is ~8×
- **Goal:** 1690 mN at 35kV (10× baseline 169 mN)
- **Result:** Realistic DIY ceiling is ~1355 mN (8×) via ducted tandem 2-stage at 8mm gap with 50W
- Open tandem (Plasma Channel data): +25%/stage only (diminishing returns from "same column of air")
- **Ducted tandem** (Drew & Follmer 2021): near-linear scaling if properly channeled — η_duct ≈ 0.85

### K Calibration (Empirical)
- K_our_NACA_build = 0.110 (calibrated from 169 mN at 35kV/20mm/500mm)
- K_NACA_theoretical = 0.623 (Nanjing 2025 paper — precision emitter)
- K_wire_mesh = 0.085 (0.77× our NACA build — mesh less efficient than foil)
- Steel wool texture: ×1.8 amplification factor

### Architecture Breakthroughs from Papers
1. **Ducted tandem** — proper inter-stage ducting enables near-linear scaling (not +25%)
2. **Wire-to-mesh** — most documented DIY geometry (Tampouris 2023)
3. **3D texture (steel wool)** — amplifies thrust 1.5-3× without HV change
4. **Wire-cylinder-plate** (Martins 2012) — cylindrical shell channels ions, 0.35 N/m thrust density
